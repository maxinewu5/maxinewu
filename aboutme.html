About Me

Who I Am

Hi! I'm Maxine Wu, just a kid who trying to survive in a tough world. I'm passionate about using technology to create meaningful change on the world. I enjoy using my problem solving skills both in theoretical and practical applications.

My Interests

At American, I'm involved with VEX Robotics Thing One and Girls Who Code. I really love being able to learn more about programming and meet others who share my love of technology. It's rewarding to be able to build cool things like robots with your friends. I also volunteer to teach math at my elementary school's Math Olympiad program.

My Vacations

Most winters, if I can, I try to head up to Tahoe to ski (and occasionally snowboard, though I haven't mastered the skill of being able to turn without falling over completely yet). There's just something satisfying about a long, hard day of skiing.
